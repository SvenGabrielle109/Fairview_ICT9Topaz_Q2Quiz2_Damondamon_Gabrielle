About the Robotics Club

This school allows students to develop construction projects with technology in this time of era. Students show most interest in learning and discovering in speaking to robots


The purpose of Robotics Club

Our purpose is to educated the students in coding and writing commands for the robots through play-action. We give and helped the students to showcase their interest in technology.
